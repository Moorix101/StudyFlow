üìö Essential Books

Make It Stick

By Peter Brown, Henry Roediger, Mark McDaniel

The definitive guide to evidence-based learning strategies. Explains why retrieval practice, spacing, and interleaving work so well. Must-read for serious learners.

A Mind for Numbers

By Barbara Oakley

How to excel at math and science even if you flunked algebra. Explains focused and diffuse thinking modes, chunking, and overcoming procrastination.

Ultralearning

By Scott Young

Strategies for rapid skill acquisition. Learn how to master hard skills quickly through meta-learning, direct practice, and feedback loops.

Deep Work

By Cal Newport

Rules for focused success in a distracted world. Learn to work deeply, eliminate shallow work, and create systems for sustained focus.
